Courses

Fortnite


In this course you will learn how to become better in playing Fortnite.
We will teach you some build strategies.
You will learn when is the time to shoot and when is the time to build.
You will also learn when is the right time to be offensive, and when is the right time to be defensive.
This course will take two months to complete.
The age group for this course is from 9-18 years.

Fall Guys


In this course you will learn how to become better in Fall Guys.
We will teach you what to do for every minigame.
You will learn the easiest and fastest ways to win each minigame.
You will learn ways to increase your speed in completing each challenge.
Also different ways to stop other players from winning will be taught.
This course will take three months to complete.
The age group for this course is from 9-18 years.

Rocket League

In this course you will learn how to become better in Rocket League.
We will teach you some team strategies and solo strategies.
You will learn different ways to maneuver your car.
You will also learn when is the right time to strike and attack opponents
And when is the best time to use your rocket boost
This course will take two months to complete.
The age group for this course is from 9-18 years.
