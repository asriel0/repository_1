About Us


These classes will help you at becoming better at the game you play.
It can help you become faster at your reflex skills and make you more aware on what is going on in the game.
We even account for your playstyle when coming up with a good strategy for you.
During your class we will talk to you on your playstyle and how it can affect playing with people of different playstyles.
These classes will be taken using Zoom. ID will be given after you have registered, by your respective teacher.
Students should have downloaded the game for which course you are attending.
